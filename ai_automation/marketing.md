# Marketing Plan: Selling Your AI-Generated Content & Services

## 1. Targeted Audience
- **Affiliate Marketers:** Busy niche site owners who need high-volume, quality content.
- **E-commerce Stores:** Shopify/Etsy sellers needing high-converting product descriptions (Tip 33).
- **SaaS Companies:** Brands that need consistent technical blog posts (Tip 03).

## 2. Marketing Channels
### Social Repurposing (Tip 02)
- Turn every SEO article into a:
    - 10-post Twitter/X thread (Tip 08).
    - LinkedIn "authority" post (Tip 02).
    - 1-minute YouTube script (Tip 07) for faceless channels.

### Outreach Strategy (Tip 35)
- Use the **Sales Email Personalizer** to scan competitor sites.
- Identify "content-poor" sections on their blogs.
- Send personalized offers with a "Free SEO Audit" (Tip 13).

## 3. Product Packages (Revenue Streams)
1. **The SEO Boost:** 4 long-form articles/month + 10 social posts.
2. **Product Polish:** 20 conversion-optimized descriptions for e-commerce.
3. **The Authority Engine:** Full market research report + competitor tracking (Tip 11).

## 4. Scaling the Sales Cycle (Tip 09 & 31)
- Use **Proposal Generator** for instant client quotes.
- Automate client onboarding with **Onboarding Docs** (Tip 32).
- Create a **Knowledge Base** (Tip 38) to reduce support time.
